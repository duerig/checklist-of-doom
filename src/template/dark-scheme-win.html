The World Trembles

As the graviton gun moves the moon into position, the Earth itself shudders under the strain. Slowly, slowly, the sun begins to disappear. First a sliver, then a bite, then it is gone.

In the eerie twilight, the leaders of the world confer and ultimately surrender. With one million dollars in hand, you relent and shut down the graviton gun.

Sunlight returns, and the world sighs with relief.
